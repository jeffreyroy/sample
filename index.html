Executive Committee

The governing body for the entire Chapter. Meets the second Monday of each month. Nine members are elected through annual elections by Chapter members (five to be elected in odd-numbered years and four in even-numbered years), plus one representative from each Chapter group selected annually. No at-large member may serve as a voting member of the ExCom for more than six consecutive years.
